The Best Movies According to Judith

My top 3 movies of all time:

The Passion of the Christ

The movie shows the story of Jesus Christ's arrest, trials and suffering, and his execution by crucifixion. It is a very hard movie to watch, but it's a movie everyone should see at least once.

The Notebook

Every girl dream of meeting someone who loves her most and whom she loves the most. The movie is romantic, tragic, and courageous.

The Curious Case of Benjamin Button

A very interesting movie showing the beginning and end of life doesn't really matter as much as the middle.
